VIM

VIM is probably my most interesting technology choice. A good friend of mine used to always use VIM, and I would be in awe just watching him jump around the document. I had made my mind up pretty quickly that if programming seemed like something that I would continue to be interested in, that I would learn VIM. Right after I created my chess game, I started researching everything I could about VIM. After about a week of VIM tutorial hell and messing with it in VScode, I took the plunge and started using it exclusively. It was quite annoying at first. I was moving at a snails pace but now I love VIM and move at a good speed. It's mostly just the motions I love, I would be open to using VIM motions in vscode or another IDE.
